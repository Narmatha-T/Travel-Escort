Kodagu

Coorg, officially known as Kodagu, is the richest hill station in Karnataka. It is famous for its stunning exotic landscapes and lush greenery. Forest covered hills, spice and coffee plantations only add to the view. Madikeri is the center point of the region with all means of transportation to move from here. Nestled in the towering mountains of Karnataka with an ever-foggy landscape, Coorg is a hill station famous for producing coffee. It is famous for its beautiful green hills and the river that flows through it. It is also a popular destination for its culture and people.

  • Captial city of Karnataka, Bangalore is 242Kms from Coorg by road.
  • It takes approximately 6 hours to reach Coorg via road
  • Coorg is located 206kms away from Bangalore aerially
  • The nearest airport to Coorg is Bajpe airport
Do not miss out on the key attractions of this beautiful hillstation location and discover the wonderful concept of "homestays" to make your experience unforgettable!

The best period to visit Coorg is during the summer months between March and June when the day time temperatures are comfortably hot.

Local taxis, jeeps, and autos provide transportation from Coorg City in Madikeri district.
